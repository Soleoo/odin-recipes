Peanut Butter Whipped Cream

Description

A light, fluffy whipped cream, that's perfect for topping for a cake or just by itself.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • ⅓ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine cream, powdered sugar, peanut butter, and vanilla extract in a bowl.
  2. Beat with an electric mixer on high speed until stiff peaks form, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Store in the refrigerator.
